What is the Bonn Integration Bee?

On Friday, the 08th of May 2026, you have the chance to prove your integral solving skills at the Bonn Integration Bee – or BIBee for short. It all starts at 4:00 PM in the small and large lecture halls at Wegelerstraße 10. First, all participants compete in a Kahoot quiz to secure a spot in the knockout round, then exciting 1-on-1 duels determine the winner – until the best is crowned in the grand finale.

If you're wondering what exactly to expect, you can look up information about the MIT Integration Bee – the event that inspired us!

Everyone is welcome to participate! You can find the exact rules and more information here.

Spectators are of course also warmly welcome!

Of course, there are great prizes to be won!

Registration

Starting from the second Bonn Integration Bee in spring 2025, no registration is required - just come by and join the Kahoot!
